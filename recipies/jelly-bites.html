Cheesy Pepper Jelly Bites

Description

Cheesy Pepper Jelly Bites are a delicious and easy appetizer that combines the tangy sweetness of jelly with the savory richness of cheese and the kick of pepper. These bites are perfect for parties, gatherings, or as a snack on a quiet evening.

The combination of cheese and jelly creates a unique flavor profile that is both sweet and savory, while the pepper adds a spicy twist that makes each bite exciting.

Ingredients

  • Cheese: Use a mild cheese like mozzarella or cheddar for best results.
  • Pepper Jelly: Choose a high-quality jelly with a good balance of sweetness and tanginess.
  • Peppers: Use fresh bell peppers for a mild, sweet flavor or spicy jalapeños for an extra kick.
  • Bread Crumbs: For coating the bites before baking.
  • Oil: For frying or roasting the bites.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the cheese into small cubes and set aside.
  3. Cut peppers into small strips and set aside.
  4. In a bowl, mix together the cheese cubes, pepper strips, and jelly until well combined.
  5. Roll each mixture into small balls and coat them in bread crumbs.
  6. Fry or bake until golden brown and crispy on the outside.
  7. Serve warm with additional jelly on top if desired.
